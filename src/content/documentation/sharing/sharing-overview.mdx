# Team Sharing

iwashere allows you to share notes with teammates securely using end-to-end encryption.

## How it works

1. **You share** a note with a teammate's email.
2. **iwashere encrypts** the note with a per-note key.
3. **The key is encrypted** for the recipient.
4. **Both files** are saved in `.iwashere-shared/` (can be committed to git).
5. **Recipient pulls** and decrypts with their email.

## Security

- Notes are encrypted with AES‑256.
- Each note has its own random key.
- Keys are encrypted for each recipient.
- Your private notes never leave your machine.
- Team members can only decrypt notes shared with them.

## Commands

- [`share`](/documentation/share) – Share notes with teammates
- [`show-shared`](/documentation/show-shared) – View notes shared with you
